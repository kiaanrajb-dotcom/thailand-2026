💱 Currency

1 THB = ~Rs. 2.89. Exchange at SuperRich or airport kiosk. Carry cash for markets + temples.

👗 Grand Palace Dress Code

Shoulders AND knees covered — both genders. No sleeveless, no shorts. Sarongs available to borrow.

🚇 Transport

Grab app = most important. BTS tokens THB 20–60/trip. Keep 100 THB coins for BTS machines.

🌧️ Weather

Monsoon June–July. Rain afternoons/evenings. Mornings fine. Carry rain jacket. 28–33°C · 85% humidity.

🛍 Shopping Tips

Balenciaga + Burberry at SPO FIRST (only Thailand outlet). The1 Tourist Card at Central Village (bring passport). Chatuchak before 11am.
